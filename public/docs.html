Admin Dashboard Documentation

Welcome to BLT Newsletter Admin

The BLT Newsletter Admin Dashboard is your complete solution for managing newsletters, subscribers, and campaigns. This guide will help you understand each feature and get the most out of the platform.

Access your dashboard: Login with your credentials to manage your newsletter operations.

Core Features

Overview

Monitor your newsletter health with key metrics including active users, new subscribers, and recent activity. Get a quick snapshot of your campaign performance and engagement.

Users

Manage your subscriber list with advanced filtering by status (Active, Bounced, Unsubscribed, Suppressed). Search users, view details, import new subscribers, and export your list.

Issues

Create and manage newsletter content with multiple status options. Draft, publish, schedule, and send issues. Track delivery metrics and engagement for each newsletter edition.

Campaigns

View all active and completed campaigns with detailed delivery statistics, bounce rates, and performance metrics. Track campaign success and analyze subscriber engagement.

Template

Manage your reusable newsletter template. Standardize your content delivery and ensure consistent branding across all editions with a professional, customizable template.

Settings

Configure email providers, set up sender identity, and manage critical system settings. Ensure proper delivery by configuring your SMTP and other essential parameters.

Managing Users

Viewing Subscribers

Navigate to the Users page to see all your subscribers. The page displays:

  • Name & Email: Complete contact information
  • Status: Active, Bounced, Unsubscribed, or Suppressed
  • Source: Where the subscriber came from
  • Subscription Date: When they joined

Filtering & Searching

Use the search bar to find users by name or email. Click status buttons to filter by subscriber type. The dashboard shows real-time counts for each category.

Importing & Exporting

Click the Import button to add new subscribers in bulk. Use Export to download your complete subscriber list as a CSV file for backup or analysis.

Creating Issues (Newsletters)

New Issue Workflow

Click New issue to start creating a newsletter edition:

  • Add a Subject - appears in subscriber inboxes
  • Write Body content using Markdown format
  • Preview your email in real-time as you edit
  • Save as draft or send immediately

Issue Status

Each issue can have one of these statuses:

  • Draft: Saved but not sent. Continue editing anytime.
  • Published: Released to all active subscribers
  • Sent: Successfully delivered to the audience
  • Scheduled: Queued for automatic delivery at a future date

Testing Before Send

Use the Send test email button to preview your newsletter in your own inbox before sending to all subscribers. This ensures formatting and content appear correctly.

Tracking Campaigns

The Campaigns page shows all newsletter sends with detailed metrics:

  • Sent: Total emails delivered
  • Failed: Bounces and delivery errors
  • Engagement: Open rates and click rates
  • Status: Delivery progress and completion

Click on any campaign to see detailed information including a list of failed deliveries, subscriber engagement details, and recommendations for improvement.

Using Templates

The Template page displays your reusable newsletter template. This ensures consistent branding and structure across all issues.

The template includes:

  • Header with your logo and branding
  • Standard sections for content organization
  • Footer with unsubscribe and contact information
  • Mobile-responsive design for all devices

Use the Use template button when creating new issues to apply this design automatically.

Settings & Configuration

Email Provider Setup

Configure your email delivery service in Settings. Supported providers include major SMTP services that handle email delivery to your subscribers.

Sender Identity

Set up your sender email address and name. This appears in subscriber inboxes as the "From" address. Verify ownership of the email address for best deliverability.

Danger Zone

This section contains critical operations that cannot be undone:

  • Clear Analytics: Reset all campaign statistics and metrics
  • Delete All Drafts: Remove all unsent newsletter drafts

Use these options with caution - they cannot be reversed.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Always test first: Use "Send test email" before sending campaigns to your full audience
  • Save drafts: Don't lose work - save as draft before sending
  • Monitor metrics: Check campaign performance regularly to understand subscriber engagement
  • Keep list clean: Regularly review bounced and unsubscribed users to maintain deliverability
  • Use templates: Maintain consistent branding by using the template for all issues
  • Regular backups: Export your subscriber list periodically for data safety

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I send a newsletter to all subscribers?

Create a new issue, write your content, click "Send campaign", and confirm. The newsletter will be sent to all active subscribers.

Can I schedule newsletters for later?

Yes! When creating an issue, select "Scheduled" status and choose your desired send time. The system will automatically send it at the specified time.

How often should I check the dashboard?

We recommend checking the Overview page daily to monitor subscriber health and engagement. Check individual campaigns after sending to track performance.

What does "Bounced" status mean?

Bounced means the email could not be delivered to that address. This could be due to an invalid email, full mailbox, or server issues.

Can I undo a sent campaign?

No, once a campaign is sent, it cannot be recalled. Always use the test email feature before sending to your full audience.

How do I contact support?

For technical support, reach out to the BLT team directly. Most issues can be resolved through the admin dashboard settings.
